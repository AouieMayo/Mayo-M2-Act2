The Colors Of The Rainbow ( R O Y G B I V )

Red Red is at the long wavelength end of the visible spectrum of light, next to orange and opposite violet. Blood is an example of the color red.

Orange Orange is the colour between yellow and red on the spectrum of visible light. Orange is an example of the color orange.

Yellow Yellow is the color between green and orange on the spectrum of light. Lemon is an example of the color yellow.

Green Green is the color between cyan and yellow on the visible spectrum. Grass is an example of the color green.

Blue Blue is the colour of light between violet and cyan on the visible spectrum. The Sky is an example of the color blue.

Indigo Indigo is a deep color close to the color wheel blue as well as to some variants of ultramarine. Indigo jeans is an example of the color indigo.

Violet Violet is the color of light at the short wavelength end of the visible spectrum, between blue and invisible ultraviolet. An Iris Flower is an example of the color violet.
